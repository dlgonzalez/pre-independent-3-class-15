🛡️ Testing Survival Guide

  • 1. Check Mic: Ensure you are in a quiet room.
  • 2. Normal Pace: Don't try to speak artificially fast. Speak at a comfortable, steady rhythm.
  • 3. Don't Over-Enunciate: English links words together (e.g., "cup of tea" sounds like "cuppa tea"). Don't pause between every single word!
  • 4. Breathe: It's just a machine. Relax!

🎯 Phonetics Focus for Today

While completing the activities on the site, focus heavily on:

  • Word Stress: Hitting the correct syllable (e.g., pho-TOG-ra-pher, not PHO-to-graph-er).
  • Linked Speech: Connecting consonants to vowels (e.g., "Look at it" sounds like "Loo-ka-tit").
